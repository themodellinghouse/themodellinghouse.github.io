The Modelling House is a New Zealand based agency providing expertise in numerical modelling, environmental data science and geospatial analysis.

We empower governmental, non-governmental and non-for-profit organisations worlwide with mathematical modelling solutions, big data analysis, academic writing and data visualisation. We believe in data science for social and environmental good, working towards a smarter, fairer and healthier planet.
